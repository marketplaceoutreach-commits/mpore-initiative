Founded in July 2024 by Niyigena Denise and Ndatimana Theophile, MPORE INITIATIVE RWANDA is a grassroots organization dedicated to empowering vulnerable children, youth, and women. We work to improve access to education, prevent drug abuse, protect childrenâ€™s rights, and create economic opportunities through skills training, entrepreneurship, and sustainable agriculture. By promoting empowerment, social equity, and community resilience, we strive to build a brighter and more sustainable future for Rwandan communities.
MPORE INITIATIVE RWANDA is dedicated to improving the lives of vulnerable populations through education, skills development, entrepreneurship, agriculture, and community engagement. We work to: Support underprivileged children in accessing education and protection. Equip youth and women with market-relevant skills, mentorship, and financial literacy. Promote sustainable agriculture, culture, and sports as pathways to community growth. Create inclusive economic opportunities that reduce poverty and enhance resilience. Our mission is to empower individuals and communities to unlock their potential and create lasting, positive change in Rwanda.
We envision a future where these young mothers transform their challenges into stepping stones toward success, inspiring others with their courage and determination. By expanding this initiative with support in materials, tools, marketing, and mentorship, we aim to create a self-sustaining program that uplifts families for generations. Our vision is to see communities strengthened by women who are confident, skilled, and economically empowered, contributing not only to their own households but also to the wider society.

Organizational Values

At Mpore initiative, we're guided by the following core values

Empowerment

We believe in equipping individuals with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to shape their own futures.

Collaboration

We foster strong partnerships with communities, organizations, and stakeholders to achieve collective impact.

Sustainability

We are committed to developing solutions that are environmentally, economically, and socially sustainable in the long term.

Integrity

We operate with transparency, accountability, and ethical conduct in all our endeavors.

Innovation

We embrace creative and adaptive approaches to address the evolving needs of Rwandan communities.

Compassion

We approach every individual with empathy, respect, and dignity, ensuring that care and understanding are at the heart of all we do.
